## Overview

RollCall connects people around shared hobbies and activities — board games, video games, hiking, sports, and more. Every new account starts with a **30-day free trial** (no credit card required) with full access to the platform. After the trial, accounts continue on the plan that fits how they use RollCall.

## Participant Plan — $10/month

For members who want to join, discover, and participate in groups they love.

**Includes:**
- Join unlimited groups
- RSVP to any event or session
- Full access to group forums and chat
- Badge and rank progression system
- Direct messages with other members
- Interactive map to discover local groups
- Partner discount program access (F100)
- Personalized home dashboard and recommendations
- Profile customization with gamer handles and availability grid
- Mobile-optimized experience (PWA)

---

## Organizer Plan — $15/month

Everything in Participant, plus the tools to build and run your own community.

**Includes everything in Participant, plus:**
- Create and host unlimited groups
- Schedule sessions and manage your group calendar
- Organizer analytics dashboard — member growth, attendance trends, engagement metrics
- Group image and identity customization
- Member join request management and approval flow
- Group waitlist management
- Co-moderator assignment
- Session templates and recurring event management
- Verified Organizer badge eligibility (admin-reviewed)
- White-glove onboarding support (first 30 days)

## Billing Cycles

### Monthly
Pay month-to-month with no commitment. Cancel any time — access continues through the end of the current billing period.

### Annual (Save 15%)
Pay once per year at a 15% discount. Annual billing locks in the lower rate and renews automatically. Cancel before renewal to avoid the next charge.

| Plan | Monthly rate | Annual rate | Billed | Annual savings |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Participant | $10.00/mo | $8.50/mo | $102.00/yr | $18.00 |
| Organizer | $15.00/mo | $12.75/mo | $153.00/yr | $27.00 |

## Free Trial

Every new account — Participant or Organizer — receives a **30-day free trial** with full platform access.

- No credit card required to start
- Full feature access from day one (including group creation for Organizer trial accounts)
- Trial ends automatically after 30 days; account prompts for a plan selection
- If no plan is selected, account access is paused (data is retained for 60 days)
- Trial cannot be extended or restarted on the same account

---

## What RollCall Never Does

RollCall subscription fees are the only charges on this platform.

- **No per-event fees** — attending any session is always included in your plan
- **No organizer-to-member charges** — organizers are prohibited from collecting fees from members; doing so results in permanent ban (F108 Community Standards)
- **No ads** — RollCall does not display advertisements or accept advertiser payment to influence content
- **No surprise charges** — pricing is exactly as listed; billing is transparent

## Competitive Positioning

| Platform | Cost to Organize | Per-Ticket Fees | Features |
|---|---|---|---|
| **RollCall** | $15/mo (or $12.75/mo annual) | None — ever | Full community platform, analytics, scheduling, forum, chat, map, badges |
| Meetup | $29.99/mo | Yes (Meetup takes a cut of paid events) | Basic group tools, limited community features |
| Eventbrite | Free to list | 3.7% + $1.79 per ticket | Event ticketing only, no ongoing community tools |
| Facebook Groups | Free | None | Basic group, no event scheduling, no gaming focus, algorithm-controlled reach |
| Discord | Free | None | Chat-focused, no discovery, no scheduling, no meetup support |

**RollCall is ~50% cheaper than Meetup** with no per-event fees, a gaming-first feature set, and a community built around recurring hobbyist groups rather than one-off events.
